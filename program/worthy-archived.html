About Worthy

Unconditional love and support, without strings attached.

The Worthy label was born out of necessity. Other programs offer conditional support; available for only those who fit a certain demographic, pay for it or jump through hoops.

Our ethos is that everyone is Worthy of love and belonging, without any strings attached.

Our Eden Valley clinicians are regularly adding or hosting Worthy Workshops on a variety of different topics. These are meant to equip young adults with the tools and resources they need to feel supported and get any help they need.
