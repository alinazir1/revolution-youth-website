A Youth mindfulness & wellness weekend

Register

#WorthyWknd
Build relationships you're proud of.

Healthy and meaningful connections with others begin with inner confidence and knowing oneself. Attend a free mindfulness weekend, aimed at growing self-confidence and reducing relationship anxiety. Become the friend, partner and person you’ve always wanted to be, for yourself, and for those you care about the most.
‍
Youth in the greater Burlington area are welcome to participate in our free mindfulness and wellness workshop. This evidence-based program offers intensive training that assists youth in developing a healthy, liberating and resilient framework around their emotional and physical connections with others.
‍
“Worthy” weekends take place throughout downtown Burlington, Vermont. We also have remote viewing available on request. Each day includes a meal, snacks, prizes, activities, games, interactive multimedia trainings, and outdoor social gatherings.
‍
This event is overseen by Eden Valley's team of mental health professionals, and attendance is encouraged for youth and young adults seeking healthier mindsets, and reduced anxiety around relationships with others. 
‍
Limited space available, registration required. For ages 13-25. This is a safe space for all, and everyone is welcome.

About Worthy

Unconditional love and support, without strings attached.

The Worthy label was born out of necessity. Other programs offer conditional support; available for only those who fit a certain demographic, pay for it or jump through hoops.

Our ethos is that everyone is Worthy of love and belonging, without any strings attached.

Our Eden Valley clinicians are regularly adding or hosting Worthy Workshops on a variety of different topics. These are meant to equip young adults with the tools and resources they need to feel supported and get any help they need.
