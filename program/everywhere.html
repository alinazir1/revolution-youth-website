FAQ’s

These are a few of the most popular questions we get about Expeditions.

Where is the application form?

Join our Everywhere Insider notifications email list for a link to apply for an upcoming Expedition.
How expensive are these trips?

Expenses obviously shift depending on location, accommodations and duration of each trip. The costs are communicated to Insiders, so sign up to get notified!
What's the age range?

Students ages 16 - 25 are welcome to apply to join a trip. We always have Host supervision and ensure all activities are legal and appropriate. We often split our Expeditions up into field-trip styled tours, and group similar ages together whenever possible.

What countries do you go?

We are open to planning trips and logistics to most any country on the US State Dept's recommended travel list. We re-visit a lot of the same countries (especially in Europe), but each year mix up our tours for new countries and experiences whenever possible.
What about COVID-19?

As of now (1/1/22) we are requiring all students who join an Expedition to have been fully vaccinated at least one month prior to our Expedition leaving. We also require (and assist with) getting COVID tests before and after travel, and as-needed when crossing international borders.
How can I pay for my trip?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, Paypal, Venmo or a check. The finances will be pre-arranged entirely before the trip so there's no confusion while we're away. For financial support please contact everywhere@revyouth.com.

Additionally we offer assistance with fundraising for Expeditions that include an Endeavour (nonprofit service) and payment plans for those who can't afford the cost in one lump sum.

Real impact, really.

Our team is very careful what kind of partnerships and volunteer opportunities we curate into an Endeavor. We're not interested in slapping some paint on a wall and posing for a photo-op with impoverished people; calling it 'missions'.

Too often people of privilege go to impoverished places and leave it no better off than when they found it. Trust us, we hate the 'white savior complex' as much as you do.
We partner with NGOs that have a local reputation of making a difference that matters in their community, and we assist where it will help them most.
Endeavors are for young adults who need a wake up call to the reality of many developing nations and people who live in suffering.
